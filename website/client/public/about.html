About Us

I Built This Because I Lived It.

I'm Marita. I'm based on the Mornington Peninsula in Victoria, and I've been working in the NDIS sector long enough to know exactly where the pain is.

Here's what I kept seeing: support workers do the care work, write rough notes at the end of a long shift, and then someone in the office has to spend hours turning those notes into something that's actually audit-ready. That person — the one doing all the rewriting — is the one I built RiteDoc for.

The documentation burden in NDIS organisations is real. It's not just a minor inconvenience. It's hours every week, it's stress before every audit, and it's a problem that nobody had built a proper solution for. So I built one.

RiteDoc takes the raw notes from your support workers and rewrites them into audit-prepared drafts — automatically. No cloud uploads, no complicated setup. Just a desktop app that does the job and keeps your participant data exactly where it should be: on your device.

I'm not a tech company. I'm someone from the sector who got frustrated enough to do something about it.
