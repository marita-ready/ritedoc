How It Works

Three Steps. That's It.

No complicated setup, no cloud accounts, no training required. Export, import, done.

01

Export CSV from Your Platform

Your admin team exports the support worker progress notes from your NDIS platform (ShiftCare, Brevity, Lumary, etc.) as a CSV file.

02

Import into RiteDoc

Drop the CSV into RiteDoc. It reads each note and rewrites it into an audit-prepared draft — automatically, offline, on your device.

03

Copy Drafts Back

Use the platform-aware clipboard to copy each rewritten draft and paste it straight back into your NDIS platform. Done.

Smart Scoring

Traffic-Light Scoring

Every note gets a traffic-light score so your admin team knows exactly where things stand — at a glance.

Green — Good to Go

The note is audit-prepared and ready. Your admin team can copy it straight back into the platform with confidence.

Orange — Missing Data

Something's missing from the original note. RiteDoc prompts you to fill in what's needed so the draft is complete before it goes back.

Red — Incident Occurred

An incident was detected in the note. All required incident documentation is filled in and ready for you — flagged clearly so nothing gets missed.
