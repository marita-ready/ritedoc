How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

No new software to learn. No changes to how your team works. Just better notes, faster.

01
Export your notes

Export your progress notes from your existing platform — ShiftCare, Brevity, Lumary, SupportAbility, or any spreadsheet. No changes to your workflow.

02
RiteDoc rewrites them

Our technology-assisted application processes your notes against current NDIS Practice Standards and returns audit-prepared drafts — including pre-filled incident documentation where required.

03
You review and approve

Every draft is returned to you for final review and approval. Your team remains in control. RiteDoc handles the drafting. You handle the sign-off.

Why RiteDoc

Built for the reality of NDIS administration.

Your admin team did not sign up to rewrite progress notes every day. RiteDoc gives them their time back.

🔒
Processed entirely on your system

RiteDoc runs directly on your computer. Everything is processed locally — no participant information is transmitted anywhere. Your data stays exactly where it belongs. With you.

📋
NDIS Practice Standards built in

Every rewrite is checked against current NDIS Practice Standards. Goal linkage. Objective language. Necessity. Participant response. All compliance requirements addressed in every draft.

🚨
Red flags caught automatically

Incidents, medication errors, restrictive practices, and integrity risks are detected automatically. Pre-filled documentation generated and returned alongside your documentation drafts.

⚡
Works with your existing platforms

ShiftCare, Brevity, Lumary, Astalty, SupportAbility, or a spreadsheet — RiteDoc works alongside whatever platform your team already uses. No migrations. No integrations. No disruption.
