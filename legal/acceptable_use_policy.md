# RiteDoc Acceptable Use Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the permitted and prohibited uses of the RiteDoc software application provided by ReadyCompliant. By installing, accessing, or using RiteDoc, you agree to comply with this AUP.

## 1. Permitted Use

RiteDoc is designed exclusively for National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) providers to process and prepare support worker progress notes for compliance and audit readiness.

You may use RiteDoc to:

*   Import raw progress notes exported from your CRM or client management system.
*   Process these notes through the local compliance engine to generate audit-prepared drafts.
*   Review the internal traffic light indicators (GREEN, ORANGE, RED) to identify compliance risks.
*   Export the processed drafts for re-import into your CRM.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

You agree not to engage in any of the following prohibited activities:

### 2.1 Reverse Engineering and Modification

You may not reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive the source code, underlying algorithms, or the local AI model (Nanoclaw/llama.cpp) of the RiteDoc software. You may not modify, adapt, translate, or create derivative works based on the software or its state cartridges.

### 2.2 Licence Abuse

Your licence key is granted on a per-seat basis and is strictly non-transferable. You may not share, sell, resell, rent, lease, or distribute your licence key or access to the software to any other individual, entity, or device without explicit written authorisation from ReadyCompliant.

### 2.3 Non-NDIS Use

RiteDoc is specifically calibrated for NDIS compliance. You may not use the software for processing data, documents, or notes unrelated to NDIS support services. This includes, but is not limited to, processing medical records, financial data, or general business correspondence.

### 2.4 Data Extraction and Scraping

You may not attempt to extract, scrape, or harvest the internal compliance scoring logic, the state-specific compliance cartridges (JSON files), or the AI model's weights and parameters. The internal 5-pillar scoring system is proprietary and must not be extracted or reverse-engineered.

### 2.5 Interference and Disruption

You may not interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of the software, its licensing validation system, or the automated support infrastructure (including the self-fix system, Dify bot, and Retell phone support). You may not attempt to bypass or disable any security features or usage limits implemented within the software.

## 3. Enforcement and Termination

ReadyCompliant reserves the right to monitor compliance with this AUP through the minimal telemetry data collected during licence validation and hardware checks.

If we determine, in our sole discretion, that you have violated this AUP, we may take immediate action, including but not limited to:

*   Issuing a warning or formal notice of breach.
*   Suspending your access to the RiteDoc software.
*   Terminating your licence key and this Agreement without refund.
*   Pursuing legal action for damages or injunctive relief.

## 4. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this AUP by another user or entity, please report it immediately to our support team at support@readycompliant.com.au.
