## 1. Service Description

RiteDoc is an offline desktop application designed to assist National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) providers in processing and preparing support worker progress notes for compliance and audit purposes. The software utilises a local compliance engine to rewrite raw notes into audit-prepared drafts, apply state-specific compliance cartridges, and assign internal traffic light indicators (GREEN, ORANGE, RED) based on compliance risk.

All processing occurs entirely offline on the Client's local machine. RiteDoc does not transmit, upload, or store any participant data, progress notes, or personally identifiable information (PII) to any cloud server or external database.

## 4. Data Handling and Privacy

ReadyCompliant is committed to the absolute privacy and security of your data. The RiteDoc software is architected specifically to ensure that participant data never leaves your control.

*   **Offline Processing:** All data processing, including PII scrubbing and compliance engine analysis, occurs locally on the device where RiteDoc is installed.
*   **No Data Transmission:** RiteDoc does not transmit progress notes, participant details, or any processed outputs to ReadyCompliant or any third party.
*   **Local Deletion Policy:** The software enforces a strict 48-hour local auto-delete policy. All imported CSV files, processed drafts, and temporary data generated during the compliance check are permanently deleted from the local application storage 48 hours after processing.
*   **Minimal Telemetry:** The only data transmitted to ReadyCompliant consists of minimal telemetry required for licence validation, hardware capability checks (to determine Standard vs. Turbo mode), and automated crash reporting (which contains no participant data).

For comprehensive details, please refer to our Privacy Policy and Data Handling Policy.
