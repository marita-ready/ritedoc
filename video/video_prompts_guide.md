# RiteDoc Explainer Video — Prompts & Specs

## Video Specs for Email

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Size** | 1920 x 1080 (landscape) or 1080 x 1080 (square — better for email) |
| **Duration** | 60–90 seconds max |
| **Format** | MP4 |
| **File size** | Under 10MB (for email embedding) |
| **Recommended for email** | 1080 x 1080 square — displays best on mobile and desktop email clients |

## OPTION 1: Canva (Recommended — Most Control)

### How to use:
1. Go to canva.com → Create a Design → search "Video" → pick **1080 x 1080 Square Video**
2. Pick a clean white/light blue template (search "minimal" or "clean product")
3. Build 8 slides (one per screen below)
4. Set each slide to 8–10 seconds
5. Use transitions: "Fade" or "Dissolve" between slides
6. Add subtle background music from Canva's free library (search "corporate upbeat" or "inspiring minimal")
7. Export as MP4

### Slide-by-slide instructions:

**Slide 1 — Logo Reveal (8 sec)**
- White background
- Centre of screen: "RiteDoc" in huge bold font (minimum 120pt)
- "Rite" in bright blue (#2563EB), "Doc" in charcoal grey (#1E293B)
- After 2 seconds, fade in underneath: "Notes Done Right." in light grey, italic, 48pt
- Animation: fade in the logo, then fade in the tagline

**Slide 2 — The Problem / Hook (10 sec)**
- Light grey or white background
- Big bold text centred: "Your support workers write notes."
- Below it, slightly smaller: "But are they audit-ready?"
- Animation: first line appears, pause, second line fades in
- Optional: subtle icon of a clipboard with a question mark

**Slide 3 — Step 1: Export (8 sec)**
- White background
- Top: small grey text "Step 1"
- Centre: huge bold text "Export Your Notes"
- Below: simple icon of a download arrow or file icon
- One line underneath in smaller text: "From your existing platform"
- Animation: slide up from bottom

**Slide 4 — Step 2: Upload (8 sec)**
- Light blue (#EFF6FF) background
- Top: small grey text "Step 2"
- Centre: huge bold text "Upload to RiteDoc"
- Below: animated progress bar going from 0% to 100% (use Canva's animation)
- Animation: fade in, progress bar fills

**Slide 5 — Step 3: Press Start (8 sec)**
- White background
- Top: small grey text "Step 3"
- Centre: huge bold text "Press Start"
- Below: a big blue button graphic that says "Process Notes"
- Make the button pulse or glow slightly
- Animation: pop in

**Slide 6 — Step 4: Done (10 sec)**
- Light blue background
- Top: small grey text "Step 4"
- Centre: huge bold text "Notes Processed"
- Below: big green checkmark circle
- Underneath: "Audit-prepared. Compliant. Ready to go."
- Animation: checkmark draws itself in, text fades in

**Slide 7 — Step 5: Review (10 sec)**
- White background
- Top: small grey text "Step 5"
- Centre: huge bold text "Review & Approve"
- Below: three large coloured circles in a row:
  - Green = "Ready"
  - Orange = "Needs Detail"
  - Red = "Flagged"
- Each circle and label should be BIG — at least 60pt labels
- Animation: circles appear one at a time, left to right

**Slide 8 — Closing / CTA (10 sec)**
- White background with subtle blue gradient at bottom
- Centre: huge bold text "Coming Soon"
- After 3 seconds, fade in below: "Be one of the first 17 Founders"
- Below that: "Join the Waitlist"
- Animation: fade in sequence — title, then subtitle, then CTA

## OPTION 2: InVideo AI (Fastest — AI Does the Work)

### Prompt to paste into InVideo AI:

```
Create a 60-second product explainer video for a software called RiteDoc. The tagline is "Notes Done Right."

Style: Clean, minimal, professional. White and light blue backgrounds ONLY. No dark backgrounds. Big bold text that's easy to read at a glance. Modern corporate feel, like an Apple product launch.

Music: Subtle, upbeat, inspiring. Not loud.

The video should flow through these screens:

Screen 1: Logo reveal. "RiteDoc" centred on white background. "Rite" in blue, "Doc" in dark grey. Tagline "Notes Done Right." fades in underneath.

Screen 2: The hook. Big text: "Your support workers write notes. But are they audit-ready?" Make this feel like a question that hits hard.

Screen 3: Step 1 — "Export Your Notes" with a download icon. Simple. One sentence.

Screen 4: Step 2 — "Upload to RiteDoc" with a progress bar animation filling to 100%.

Screen 5: Step 3 — "Press Start" with a blue button graphic.

Screen 6: Step 4 — "Notes Processed" with a green checkmark. Text below: "Audit-prepared. Compliant. Ready to go."

Screen 7: Step 5 — "Review & Approve" showing three traffic light dots: Green (Ready), Orange (Needs Detail), Red (Flagged).

Screen 8: Closing — "Coming Soon" then "Be one of the first 17 Founders" then "Join the Waitlist"

Important: Every piece of text must be LARGE and readable in under 2 seconds. No paragraphs. No small fonts. Maximum 10 words per screen. Clean transitions between each screen. Professional and trustworthy feel throughout.
```

### Settings in InVideo:
- Audience: Business owners, NDIS providers, aged care managers
- Platform: Email / Website
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) for email, or Landscape (16:9) for website

## Tips for Making It a No-Brainer

The video sells by doing three things:

1. **Hits the pain point immediately** — "But are they audit-ready?" makes them feel the problem in their gut. Every provider worries about audits.

2. **Shows how stupidly easy it is** — 5 steps, each one sentence. Export. Upload. Start. Done. Review. That's it. The simplicity IS the sell. They think "wait, that's all I have to do?"

3. **Creates urgency at the end** — "First 17 Founders" makes them feel like they'll miss out if they don't act. Scarcity drives action.

The video doesn't need to be flashy. It needs to be CLEAR. If someone watches it on mute and still understands what RiteDoc does in 60 seconds, you've nailed it.
