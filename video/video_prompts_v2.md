| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|

## Prompt for InVideo AI / Canva AI / Higgsfield / Any AI Video Tool

```
I need a professional, compelling product explainer video for my software product. The video needs to SELL — it should hook the viewer in the first 3 seconds and make them feel like they absolutely need this product. Under 2 minutes.

ABOUT THE PRODUCT:
- Product name: RiteDoc
- Tagline: "Notes Done Right."
- Company: ReadyCompliant
- What it does: RiteDoc is a desktop app for NDIS and aged care providers in Australia. Support workers write daily progress notes about their clients, but those notes are often messy, incomplete, and not audit-ready. RiteDoc takes those raw notes and rewrites them into professional, audit-prepared documentation in minutes. It uses a traffic light system — green means ready to go, orange means needs more detail, red means flagged for review.
- Who it's for: NDIS providers, disability support organisations, aged care facilities, and compliance managers in Australia.
- The pain point: These organisations face government audits. If their notes aren't compliant, they risk losing funding, registration, or worse. Support workers aren't trained writers — they're carers. RiteDoc bridges that gap.
- How it works: Export notes from their existing platform → Upload to RiteDoc → Press Start → Notes are processed and returned audit-ready → Review using the traffic light system and approve.
- Key selling point: It's dead simple. No training needed. No internet required (runs offline for privacy). Takes minutes, not hours.

BRAND SPECS:
- Primary colour: Blue (#2563EB)
- Secondary colour: Charcoal/dark grey (#1E293B) — use sparingly for text only
- Backgrounds: Light colours ONLY — white, light blue, light grey. NO dark backgrounds. NO navy. NO black backgrounds.
- Logo text: "RiteDoc" — "Rite" in blue, "Doc" in charcoal grey
- Font style: Clean, modern sans-serif. BIG and bold. Every word must be readable at a glance by someone wearing glasses.
- Overall feel: Clean, professional, trustworthy, modern. Think Apple product launch — minimal, confident, spacious.

MUSIC:
- Subtle, upbeat, inspiring background track. Not loud. Not distracting.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Must end with "Coming Soon" and a call to action to join the waitlist
- Must make the viewer understand what RiteDoc does AND want to buy it
- Text on screen must be large and readable — no small fonts, no paragraphs, no clutter
- Keep it simple and easy to understand — the target audience are care providers, not tech people

Design the video however you think will be most compelling and effective. You're the designer — show me the best way to present this product.
```
